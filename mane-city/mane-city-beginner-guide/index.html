Mane City Beginner Guide: Start Simple, Build Smarter

A simple beginner-friendly guide to understand Mane City, resources, Daily Roar, upgrades and Competitive Mode without getting lost.

Mane City Beginner Guide: Start Simple, Build Smarter

EN

Mane City looks simple at first.

You enter the game, see your city, your businesses, your mansion and your rewards. Then, after a few minutes, you realize there is much more underneath: Cash, Diamonds, Vouchers, furniture, upgrades, Daily Roar, NFTs and Competitive Mode.

That is where many new players start feeling confused.

This is not an official guide. It is a simple gameplay journal for players who want to understand the basics before spending resources too fast.

Mane City is a tycoon-style Web3 game connected to Loaded Lions. The goal is to build your city, grow your businesses and improve your progression over time.

The first thing to understand

EN

Mane City is not only about clicking upgrades.

It is about understanding what each resource does before using it.

Cash helps you grow your city and buy upgrades.

Diamonds become important for boosters, mansion progression and special upgrades.

Vouchers become more relevant later when you start exploring Competitive Mode.

At the beginning, your job is not to optimize everything.

Your job is simply to avoid random spending.

Daily Roar matters more than it looks

EN

One of the best beginner habits is checking Daily Roar every day.

Daily rewards may look small, but progression games are built around consistency. Small rewards collected every day can become important over time.

For a new player, Daily Roar also teaches the rhythm of the game: log in, collect rewards, observe your city and upgrade carefully.

Do not ignore the easy rewards.

Your first 3 days in Mane City

EN

If you are starting today, I would not rush.

Day 1

Explore the interface. Check Daily Roar. Look at Cash, Diamonds and your first upgrade options.

Do not worry about strategy yet.

Day 2

Start observing what changes after upgrades. Does your growth improve? Does something unlock? Does your mansion progression change?

This is where the game starts becoming clearer.

Day 3

Before spending aggressively, start reading about Competitive Mode. You do not need to join every event immediately.

First, understand the difference between Normal Mode and Competitive Mode.

Normal Mode vs Competitive Mode

EN

This is one of the most important beginner concepts.

Normal Mode is your main long-term progression.

Competitive Mode is event-based and works differently.

Your main city remains your core progression, while Competitive Mode becomes a place to test strategy during temporary events.

If you are new, learn first and compete later.

The biggest beginner mistake

EN

The biggest mistake is thinking: “I need to understand everything immediately.”

You do not.

Start with:

Cash, Diamonds, Daily Roar, Upgrades, Mansion progression, Normal Mode, Competitive Mode.

Then go deeper later.

Mane City becomes much easier when you stop trying to solve everything in one session.

My personal approach

EN

If you are new, treat the first days as observation.

Check your city. Collect rewards. Observe your upgrades. Read the official Playbook.

Notice what changes after every action.

That is where the game becomes more interesting.

Because Mane City does not feel like a simple idle game. It feels more like a small economy where every resource has a role.

Final thought

EN

The best way to start Mane City is not to rush.

Start simple. Build the daily habit. Understand resources. Avoid random spending.

Then slowly move into deeper strategy.
